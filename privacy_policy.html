01

Introduction

At Shree Bhramani Industries, we prioritize the confidentiality and protection of corporate and individual data. This Privacy Policy details our operational protocols regarding the collection, processing, storage, and sharing of information when you interact with our websites, utilize our industrial CIJ/TIJ/Laser machinery, request technical assistance, or engage in enterprise B2B transactions with us.

By utilizing our online portals or industrial equipment hardware infrastructure, you consent to the governance frameworks and standard operating procedures set forth in this agreement.

02

Information We Collect

In the standard operation of supplying high-precision industrial coding machinery, we gather specific categories of structured data:

Directly Provided Content

Corporate correspondence, billing addresses, purchasing agent identities, contact phone numbers, requested ink formulations, and customized printer hardware requirements submitted via our inquiry and quotation workflows.

Machine Diagnostic Telemetry

Where smart remote-diagnostic interface modules are activated, we receive real-time device sensor readings, operating hours, fluid pressure telemetry, and printhead synchronization state to streamline preventive field support.

03

How We Use Your Data

Collected datasets are strictly deployed to reinforce the efficiency, uptime, and delivery compliance of our enterprise solutions:

  • check_circle Fulfillment & Logistics: Rapid execution of quotation requests, machine dispatches, spare part distributions, and targeted bulk consumables (inks, solvents) delivery.
  • check_circle Technical Lifecycle Maintenance: Scheduling proactive onsite servicing, field engineer allocation based on operational serial numbers, and customized machinery tuning.
  • check_circle Quality Control Enhancement: Systematic evaluation of machine hardware performance benchmarks across typical geographic and thermal packaging line conditions.
04

Data Security & Storage

We apply enterprise-grade data perimeter security to shield all digital records from uncertified entry, unauthorized alteration, or strategic loss. Client accounts, machinery registries, and technical communications are encrypted at rest using sophisticated cryptographic standards.

info

Strict Access Authorization: Physical servers hosting central client maintenance databases are strictly audited under multi-factor administrative clearance protocols. Only specialized customer service technical personnel possess access rights.

05

Third-Party Disclosures

Shree Bhramani Industries strictly opposes the commercialization, selling, or leasing of enterprise client identity databases to secondary data brokerages. Information sharing is bounded strictly to essential third-party service enablers bound by tight NDAs:

• Certified national and international cargo supply chains delivering heavy machinery payloads.
• Secure financial processing institutions ensuring safe electronic clearance of commercial POs.
• Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when compelled under unambiguous judicial mandate.

06

Cookies & Diagnostic Data

Our digital web platform integrates necessary functional cookies to retain specific UI preferences, language selections, and secure inquiry token sessions. Diagnostic analytic strings record browser type, device form-factor, and referential navigation trails to continually refine web loading speed and user responsiveness.

07

Your Corporate Rights

Authorized representatives of our enterprise clients maintain the unconditional right to access their recorded corporate file parameters, request real-time modifications to accounting destinations, or request complete un-linking of telemetry monitoring where manual stand-alone maintenance auditing is preferred.

Questions Regarding Data Safety?

Should your corporate compliance teams have specialized queries concerning GDPR compatibility, local Indian IT Act synchronization, or tailored multi-plant data segmentation, directly approach our designated technical privacy coordinators.
